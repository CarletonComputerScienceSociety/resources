Are Tutorials Mandatory?

TLDR: It is manadatory to register for a tutorial. The attendance requirement for a tutorial varies between courses.

—

It is mandatory to register for one tutorial section along with the lecture section of a course. Whether or not attendance is required for the tutorials will be mentioned in the course outline (which is released by the first lecture of the course) (1), or communicated by the instructor.
