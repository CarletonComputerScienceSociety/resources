Should I Be Worried About the Distance Between Two Buildings for Consecutive Classes?

TLDR: There is a 10 minute gap between any two consecutive classes, which is long enough to get from one classroom to the next, regardless of their locations.

—

There is a 10 minute gap between any two consecutive classes, which is long enough to get from one classroom to the next, regardless of their locations. You should identify the locations and familiarize yourself with the ideal route for each of your classes before the beginning of the semester.

You can also use the campus map (1) to find a route between two buildings.
