When Should I Take COOP 1000?

TLDR: COOP 1000 (1) is generally taken in the fall term of your second year, two semesters before your first co op work term.

—

COOP 1000 (1) is generally taken 2 semesters before your first co-op work term. If you are following the standard work-study sequence (2), you will get an email to register in COOP 1000 for the fall term of your second year, and your first work term will be in the summer after your second year.

Note that COOP 1000 is worth 0 credits and you can still take up to 2.5 credits (or 3.0 credits with an overload (3) ) worth of other courses in the same term.
